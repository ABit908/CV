    • A face recognition system is an advanced technology designed to identify and authenticate individuals by analyzing unique patterns of facial features from digital images or video streams.   • Railway Reservation System

    • The railway reservation system is a pivotal component of modern transportation infrastructure, streamlining the process of booking and managing train tickets. This system enables passengers to conveniently reserve seats or berths, check train schedules, and obtain real-time information about their journeys. The railway reservation system not only enhances the efficiency of ticket booking but also facilitates proper allocation of resources, reducing the likelihood of overbooking or discrepancies.
